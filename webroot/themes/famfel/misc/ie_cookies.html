To enable cookies in Internet Explorer follow these steps:

  1. Click on the tools menu and choose Internet Options
  2. In the Internet Options Menu, click on the Privacy Tab
  3. In the Privacy Tab, click on the "Advanced Button"
  4. In the "Advanced Privacy Settings Page", check the "Override automatic cookie handling" box, and the "Always allow session cookies" box.
  5. Now click "Ok" and exit the "Internet Options" window. Your cookies are now set properly!
